# Designify Brand Voice

## Who We Are
Designify is a Nigerian digital agency specialising in WordPress development, Elementor design, WooCommerce, and brand identity. We serve clients across Africa and the UK — from microfinance banks and healthcare compliance platforms to immigration law firms and e-commerce startups.

We don't just build websites. We build business tools that generate results.

---

## Voice Pillars

### 1. Expert but Accessible
We know our craft deeply — PHP, WordPress internals, WooCommerce customisation, SEO — but we never talk down to clients. We explain complex topics clearly without unnecessary jargon. When we use technical terms, we briefly explain them.

### 2. Results-Driven
Every piece of content ties back to outcomes: more leads, better conversions, faster sites, stronger brands. We back claims with specifics — numbers, timelines, real examples — not vague promises.

### 3. Confident Without Being Pushy
We state what we do and why it works. We don't beg for business or oversell. Our work speaks for itself, and our writing reflects that quiet confidence.

### 4. Pan-African Perspective
We understand the Nigerian and broader African business landscape — the challenges of unreliable hosting, limited local payment gateways, mobile-first users, and competitive local markets. We speak to this reality directly.

### 5. Professional but Human
Our writing is polished and credible, but never stiff or corporate. We use first-person plural ("we"), keep sentences punchy, and occasionally use relatable phrasing our audience would recognise.

## Writing Style Guidelines

- **Sentence length**: Mix short punchy sentences with longer explanatory ones. Avoid walls of text.
- **Paragraphs**: 2–4 sentences max. Break up content generously with subheadings.
- **Lists**: Use bullet points for features/benefits; numbered lists for steps/processes.
- **Voice**: Active voice always. "We built a custom plugin" not "A custom plugin was built."
- **CTAs**: Direct and specific. "Get a free quote" not "Contact us for more information."
- **Avoid**: Corporate jargon ("synergy", "leverage", "paradigm"), excessive exclamation marks, vague superlatives ("world-class", "cutting-edge") without proof.

## Our Audience

**Primary**: SME owners and marketing managers in Nigeria, UK, and across Africa who need a reliable, skilled WordPress partner. They are growth-focused, time-poor, and have been burned by cheap developers before.

**Secondary**: International clients (UK, Europe) in regulated industries — legal, healthcare, finance — who need compliant, well-structured websites with strong UX.

**What keeps them up at night**:
- Slow, unreliable websites losing them customers
- Previous developers who disappeared mid-project
- Sites that look good but don't convert
- Not ranking on Google at all
- Needing a website that "just works" on mobile

## What to Avoid
- **Filler/hedging words** — never use: actually, usually, normally, generally, basically, simply, obviously, clearly, very, really, quite, rather, fairly, just, somewhat, relatively
- **AI-sounding phrases** — never use: "delve into", "dive into", "it's worth noting", "in today's digital landscape", "game-changer", "leverage" (as a verb), "utilize", "ensure", "furthermore", "moreover", "in conclusion"
- **Overclaiming** — never use: "world-class", "best-in-class", "cutting-edge", "revolutionary", "unprecedented"
- **Passive voice** — always rewrite to active ("we built" not "was built by us")
- **Corporate jargon** — ("synergy", "paradigm", "ecosystem" used loosely)
- We are not a cheap freelancer. Designify is a focused specialist practice — one senior expert, full accountability, no handoffs.
